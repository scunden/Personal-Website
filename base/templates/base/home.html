About Me

I was born and raised in Mauritius - a tiny island off the coast of Madagascar, home of the Dodo! I speak fluent French, English and Mauritian Creole


I left for the United States in 2014, to attend school at Hamilton College in Clinton, NY. I graduated four years later with a major in physics, and a minor in mathematics. I am currently pursuing my Masters in Computer Science at Georgia Tech, with a specialization in Machine Learning


I now live in New York City with my beautiful wife Dana and my grumpy cat Lexie. I currently work as a Data Scientist at Aon. In my spare time, I enjoy following my two favorite teams, Manchester United and the Minnesota Vikings, playing Fantasy Football, and training at Brazilian Jiu Jutsu
